Incentives Program

  • The primary purpose of the incentives program is to get students across campus to collectively take part in the initiative to reduce energy consumption in campus residence halls.
  • The incentives program will be dual tiered offering a community reward and a residence hall reward.
  • If we as a community can drop energy consumption on campus by 5%, the savings will be transferred to the Programming Council for use during Spring Weekend.
  • The residents of the three buildings that reduce their consumption the most will be rewarded with $10 in Lyons Bucks.
